Privacy Policy & SMS Consent

Effective Date: May 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 1, 2025

1. About This Policy

Google Review MD ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Wildcard Tech, provides Google review management services for restaurants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, and describes the consent process for any communications we conduct.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you reply to an outreach email sent by our team. By replying to that email, you are engaging in a two-way communication with us. The information we may collect includes:

  • Your name and email address (from the email thread)
  • The content of your reply
  • The date and time of your response
  • Your restaurant or business name

3. Opt-In Consent & Verbal Consent Script

How Consent Is Obtained Consent is collected verbally and via email reply. Every outreach email sent by our team includes the following disclosure, which constitutes the opt-in consent process.

The following disclosure is included in the footer of every outreach email sent by Google Review MD:

"By replying to this email, you acknowledge that your response will be received and processed by the Google Review MD team at Wildcard Tech. Your contact information will not be sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Our team may follow up via email regarding Google review management services for your restaurant. To opt out of future communications at any time, reply with the word STOP or email us at operations@wildcard-tech.com."

No SMS messages are sent to contacts or end users. This toll-free number is used exclusively as an internal notification system — when a contact replies to an outreach email, an automated SMS is sent only to our internal team member (Myron) to alert them of the reply. The end user receives no SMS messages.

4. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through email replies is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about Google review management
  • Internally notify our team that a reply has been received
  • Follow up regarding our services for restaurants

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing, and we do not sell or rent your data to any third parties.

5. SMS Use — Internal Only

Our toll-free phone number is used exclusively for inbound internal SMS notifications. Specifically:

  • When a restaurant contact replies to an outreach email, an automated workflow triggers an SMS notification to our internal team member only.
  • No SMS is ever sent to the contact or end user.
  • The SMS contains only the content of the email reply for internal review purposes.

6. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of future communications from Google Review MD at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any email you receive from us
  • Emailing us directly at operations@wildcard-tech.com

Upon receiving your opt-out request, we will remove you from our outreach list within 5 business days.

7. Data Retention

We retain email correspondence for up to 12 months for business record purposes. If you request deletion of your data, we will honor that request within 30 days by contacting us at the email below.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our internal systems use industry-standard security practices.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party tools to operate our communications workflow:

  • Twilio — for internal SMS notification routing
  • n8n — for automated workflow processing of email replies

These services process data only as needed to deliver the internal notification. They are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.
